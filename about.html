I started my journey as a child with a love for art and no where to guide that love. It wasn't until I was long into my adult life that I started thinking about the design and architecture of websites. With a desire to get into the UI and coding trade, I began my journey. And I'm able to get my foot through the door with the Parson's School of Design UX/UI class and the SheCodes online coding course. My goals for the future are to continue growing in coding and the designing aspects of websites and apps. Eventually, I hope to get into game design and development. The tech world is developing so quickly and there's so much beauty in it. I want to add my own pieces of beauty to that world and I can't wait to see where this journey takes me!
